History of The University of Manila

The University of Manila (UM) was founded in 1913 with the goal of providing accessible and high-quality education to Filipinos. Throughout its long history, UM has grown into a respected institution known for producing graduates who excel in various fields such as law, engineering, business, and education.

Despite changes in society and technology over the years, the university continues to uphold its core values of love for country, science, and virtue. Today, UM remains committed to shaping future leaders who contribute meaningfully to nation-building.

Mission

ACE - To accomplish something with complete success.
A - Anchor in the hearts of the youth the love of country, science, and virtue.
C - Create an atmosphere of academic excellence and competence.
E - Empower the youth with modern scientific technological skills in braving the challenges of the global society.

Vision

To impart to the future generations the University of Manila trilogy of ideals: love of country, love of science, love of virtue, in making the lives of others better, brighter, lighter, and more livable by making quality education accessible to all.

Motto

To acquire knowledge, skills, and values fo the benefit of mankind.
