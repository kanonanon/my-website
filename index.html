Mission

To empower Filipinas by providing high-quality, accessible, and minimalist beauty products that enhance natural confidence.

Vision

To become a leading beauty brand in the Philippines and beyond, known for simplicity, inclusivity, and innovation.

Motto

“Simple Beauty. Real Confidence.”

Our History

BLK Cosmetics is a proudly Filipina beauty brand that focuses on simplicity, inclusivity, and everyday beauty. Since its establishment, the brand has aimed to provide high-quality, cruelty-free products designed to enhance natural features rather than cover them.

Over the years, BLK has grown into one of the most recognized local beauty brands in the Philippines. Its minimalist approach and strong identity have attracted a wide audience, especially modern Filipinas looking for effortless and practical makeup solutions.

Today, BLK continues to innovate by creating products that adapt to different lifestyles while maintaining its core philosophy of confidence through simplicity.
